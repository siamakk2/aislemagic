FAQ

Common questions

What is AisleBird and how does it work?

AisleBird is a free AI-powered shopping list app that automatically sorts your grocery list by department and physical location for your specific store. You type or speak your items, and every one lands in the correct department (Produce, Dairy, Meat, etc.) in the order you walk through that store — with a directional hint like "Near entrance" or "Back wall" on every item card.

Which stores does AisleBird support?

AisleBird supports 11 retail chains across 300+ locations: Whole Foods, Trader Joe's, Target, Walmart, Costco, CVS, Walgreens, Home Depot, Lowe's, Friedman's, and Tractor Supply. Coverage spans California, Chicago, New York, Los Angeles, Austin, Denver, Seattle, Boston, Atlanta, Phoenix, and San Diego — with more cities being added regularly.

Is AisleBird really free?

Yes — completely free with no hidden costs, no credit card required, and no trial period. All features are included: voice input, smart aisle sorting, shopping history, shared lists, budget mode, offline support, and more. A Pro plan for power users is coming soon at $3.99/month, but the free tier will always include everything you need.

Does AisleBird sort by actual aisle numbers?

AisleBird sorts by department in the order you walk through the store — not by specific aisle numbers (which vary by individual location and change frequently). Each item displays the department name, emoji, and a directional location hint like "Near entrance," "Back wall," or "Center aisles" so you always know where to head. For most shoppers, this is more useful than a number because the direction is immediately actionable.

Can I use voice to add items to my shopping list?

Yes. Tap the microphone button and speak naturally: "Add two cartons of almond milk, organic bananas, and sourdough bread." AisleBird recognizes each item, identifies its department, and sorts it into your list instantly. It also understands check-off commands like "Got the eggs" to mark items complete while your hands are full.

Does AisleBird save my shopping history?

Yes. Every time you finish shopping and remove your checked items, AisleBird automatically saves that trip — the store, date, and every item you bought. Open My History in Settings to see all past trips, filter by store chain, and expand any trip to see the full item list. You can also tap "Add all items" to instantly rebuild a past list for your current store.

Can I share my shopping list with my family?

Yes, in two ways. Invite by email (Settings → Share This List) — your partner gets live edit access to the same list. Or generate a shareable link (Settings → Share List Link) that anyone can open without creating an account. Both update in real time so you can split up in the store and still stay in sync.

Does AisleBird work without internet?

Yes. AisleBird is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with a service worker that caches the app after your first visit. Once you've opened it once, it loads and works completely offline — add items, check things off, view your list. It syncs back to the cloud the next time you have a connection.
