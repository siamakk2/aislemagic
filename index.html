Add Items

Every way you think of a list

🎤

Voice Entry

Tap the mic and say your list in natural language — "two cartons of almond milk, organic bananas, sourdough." Each item is added and sorted instantly. Also works for checking things off: "Got the eggs."

✏️

Type & Comma-Separate

Type "milk, eggs, bread, salmon" in one shot — AisleBird splits, categorizes, and sorts all of them at once. No need to add items one by one.

📋

Import from Notes

Copy your list from iPhone Notes, any text app, or a recipe — paste it in and AisleBird parses every line, identifies each item's department, and sorts your entire list in walk order automatically.

🔍

History Autocomplete

The moment you start typing, AisleBird suggests items from your personal buying history, ranked by how often you buy them. Your regulars are always one tap away.

⚡

Quick Add

Store-specific quick-add grids show your most-likely items for that chain. Whole Foods shows organic produce and bulk items; Tractor Supply shows feed and livestock supplies.

📝

Item Notes

Add a private note to any item — "get the ripest ones," "brand X only," "2 lbs." Notes show on the card and are included when you copy or share the list.

Navigate

Walk the store without backtracking

📍

Department & Location on Every Item

Every item shows its department name, emoji, and a directional location hint — "🥦 Produce · Near entrance" or "🧀 Dairy · Back wall" — so you always know where to head without scanning the whole list.

⇅

Smart Sort by Aisle

Your list is automatically sorted in the order you walk through that specific store — Produce → Bakery → Dairy → Meat → Frozen. No backtracking, no doubling back.

👆

Tap Your Current Aisle

Tap the department chip you're standing in and your remaining list instantly reorders to match your exact path from that point forward.

👉

Swipe Right to Check Off

Swipe any item card to the right to check it off — no need to tap the small checkbox. The card flashes green and the item moves to your cart. One-handed and glove-friendly.

⚠️

Out-of-Stock Alerts

Flag any item as out of stock at your store. Every other shopper sees a ⚠️ warning on that item with suggested alternatives — "Try: Oat Milk · Soy Milk." Community-sourced, real-time.

🔍

Filter Within Your List

Tap the search icon to filter your list by name as you shop. Instantly find "salmon" in a 40-item list without scrolling. Press Escape to clear and see everything again.

Share & Collaborate

Shop together, even when apart

🤝

Shared Lists

Invite your partner, roommate, or anyone by email. They open the same live list, can add items, check things off, and see every change in real time. No extra app needed.

🔄

Real-Time Sync

Using AisleBird on two devices? Changes sync instantly via Supabase Realtime. A green dot pulses to confirm a sync arrived. Shared collaborators update every 15 seconds.

📤

Copy List as Text

One tap copies your full list to the clipboard — grouped by department, with quantities and notes. Paste it into Messages, WhatsApp, or email in a second.

📋

List Templates

Save any list as a named template — "Weekly Whole Foods," "BBQ Supplies," "Renovation Run." Apply a template to any store in one tap — all items land in the right departments, already in walk order.

🔄

Re-buy Last Trip

When you clear your checked items, AisleBird saves that trip automatically. Next time your list is empty, a "Re-buy Last Trip" button restores everything in one tap.

🖨️

Print Your List

Tap the 🖨️ button (or Ctrl+P on desktop) to print a clean, formatted list grouped by department — store name, date, quantities, and notes all included.

Power Features

Built for serious shoppers

💰

Budget Mode

Enable Budget Mode in Settings and every item gets a price field pre-filled with a category estimate. Edit any price. A live running total in the progress bar shows your estimated remaining spend as you check items off.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

Toggle between a deep dark theme (default) and a warm cream light mode in Settings. Your preference persists across sessions. Both modes use full contrast for glanceability in bright store lighting.

📱

Works Offline

AisleBird is a PWA with a service worker. Once you've opened it once, it loads instantly even without internet. Your list stays on-device. Syncs to the cloud when you reconnect.

↩️

Undo Remove

Accidentally delete an item? A 4-second undo toast appears immediately. Tap Undo to restore the item to its exact position in the list. No data is ever permanently lost during a session.

⌨️

Keyboard Shortcuts

For desktop and laptop shoppers: / opens the filter, A focuses the add input, Esc closes any overlay, ⌘P prints.

🔔

Haptic Feedback

On Android and supported browsers, AisleBird vibrates subtly when you check an item off (12ms), add an item (18ms), or delete one (double pulse). Toggle it in Settings.
